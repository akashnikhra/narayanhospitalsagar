The Narayan Hospital has a strong legacy of more than 3 decades that redefines healthcare arena in a unique and larger perspective. Since its inception the group has always strived to take the healthcare services to a new level. This journey of healthcare excellence and highest level of patient satisfaction has seen many milestones on its way.Today the group boasts a panel of more than 10 doctors and an array of state-of-the-art healthcare facilities across its hospitals in Central India.
Patients at Narayan Hospital include common people, corporate, government employees and many who’s who of the society.With a passion to surpass patients’ expectations and bring about a meaningful change in the lives of people, the Narayan Hospital is on its way to becoming a leading healthcare provider of Central India.
